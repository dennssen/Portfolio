Caster Camera

Personal Project

Luau

Custom goal effect created by yasaifex integrated into the caster camera

Caster Camera Features:

  • Free Camera
  • Player POV
  • Player 3rd Person
  • Shootoff & Goal Particles
  • 3 Customizable Overviews
  • Configurable Post Processing Preset
  • Customizable Keybinds for Keyboard & Controller

The Caster Camera is an all-in-one caster tool developed for the Orion Drift spectator client that combines a free moving camera and a player follow camera along with other helpful features to make the camera man caster experience as smooth and user-friendly as possible.
