AsteroidMiner

Personal Project

C#

MonoGame

The yellow asteroid is worth the most

AsteroidMiner is an incredibly simple game that i made in about a week as part of an assignement i got when i was visiting TietoEvry card services for 2 weeks.

The assignement was given to me to try and challenge me with how object oriented programming worked, however at that time i had already become very comfortable with OOP. I still found the assignement a learning experience as it was the first time i used a game engine that did not come with a GUI for placement of game objects or hitboxes.

AsteroidMiner is a top-down survivor shooter rougelite where you control a tiny spaceship that has to destroy and avoid asteroids. Breaking asteroids gives you money that you can use to upgrade your ship for your next run. The game is heavily inspired by Nodebuster.
