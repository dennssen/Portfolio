Follow Pluss

Personal Project

Luau

3rd Person follow of the ball triggered by the auto shot detection

Follow Pluss Features:

  • Easy player searching
  • 5 Camera Modes
    • Hand Held
    • First Person
    • Head Direction (3rd)
    • Velocity Direction (3rd)
    • Face The Ball (3rd)
  • Position & Rotation Offset
  • Goalie Camera Angle
  • Auto Shot Detection / Ball Follow
  • In Game Camera Controlls with Gestures
  • Mute Self
  • Message Implementation

Follow Pluss is a highly customizable, user-friendly player follow camera script for recording yourself and your gameplay in Orion Drift using the spectator client.

Follow Pluss also serves as a framework for other developers to use for implementing player following into their own scripts. My caster camera is one such example, the caster camera uses follow pluss as a framework for the player following and then builds on top of it with it's own features.
