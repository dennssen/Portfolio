Custom Treasure Quality

Personal Project

Lua

The binding of Isaac

Mod

A quality 4 item. The best quality item in the standard game.

This mod started off as just a fun challenge for myself and a mod i actually wanted but that i couldn't find, therefore i decided to make it myself. When my friend mentioned he wanted the mod as well i figured i might as well upload it to the steam workshop. Suprisingly enough it gathered more traction than i expected, and i am very glad it did.

The Custom Treasure Quality mod was the first coding project i ever released to the public and my first time working with user feedback and pushing out updates with patch notes. It was also the first time i tried compatability with other mods such as the Mod config menu mod.

This mod has helped me out more than i think it has towards where i am today.

Custom Treasure Quality is a mod that rerolls the item in the treasure room of the first floor until it has a quality that is considered acceptable by the user. The default lowest value is 3 but with the use of Mod config menu it can be whatever the user desires.
