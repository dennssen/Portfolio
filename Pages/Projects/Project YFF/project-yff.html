Project YFF

School Project

C#

Unity 2D

The player sliding on the floor of a spaceship

As the lead developer and only programmer in our group i made almost everything in game except for the music and a couple of sprite concepts. I worked on the player movement, the gameplay, the level selector, the saving system and so on. And because i had such massive amounts of influence on the game the movement is heavily inspired by my favorite game ULTRAKILL with a tiny bit of Pizza Tower for the gameplay.

The gameplay goes as follows:
You play as a robot designed to destroy spaceships. Using your utility of wall kicks, wall jumps, slides and ground-pounds you run as fast as possible through these spaceships to get to and destroy their core. As the core blows up when you run into it at 100m/s the entire ship changes structure and you need to navigate backwards through a slightly modified version of the level you just played before your time runs out and the ship explodes with you in it.

Project YFF is an unreleased school project that i put together with some of my classmates. The goal of the project was to create a playable demo of a game with any genre that had a medium-low size. The game ended up becoming a fast-paced escape platformer with 6 playable levels.
